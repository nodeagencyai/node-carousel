Your lead magnet isn't broken. Your idea of what a lead magnet does is.

Most people treat the download as the finish line. It isn't. It's the audition for the offer that comes after. If the PDF ends with "hope this helps" and a generic nurture sequence, you just paid to teach your leads that you're forgettable.

The magnets that actually convert do one of three things: they give the reader a small win they can't unsee, they expose a problem they didn't know they had, or they make the next step feel like the only logical move. Everything else is a content marketing hobby.

The fastest win isn't a new magnet. It's rewriting what happens in the 48 hours after the download. Specific offer. One email. One link.
